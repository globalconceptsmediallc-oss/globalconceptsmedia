The GCM Growth Framework

Every customer asks five questions before they buy.

The Growth Review evaluates how clearly your business answers the five questions that influence customer decisions.

Question 1

Why should someone choose you?

We evaluate differentiation, positioning, proof, expertise, guarantees, experience, and reasons customers should choose you over alternatives.

Question 2

Why should they choose you now?

We look for urgency, timing, risk, opportunity cost, seasonal demand, offers, and reasons customers should act instead of waiting.

Question 3

Why should they trust you?

We review reviews, testimonials, photos, case studies, certifications, team information, transparent process, and credibility signals.

Question 4

What happens next?

We evaluate whether customers know exactly how to call, request a quote, schedule, visit, buy, or take the next step.

Question 5

How will success be measured?

We review whether leads, calls, traffic, rankings, conversions, revenue, and campaign results can be measured clearly.

Result

A clearer growth plan.

When these questions are answered with evidence, recommendations become easier to prioritize and measure.
