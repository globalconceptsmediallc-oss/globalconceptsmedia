The GCM Growth Framework

Every business must answer five questions before customers buy.

Technology changes. Marketing channels change. Customer decision-making does not. The Growth Review starts with these five questions before recommending SEO, ads, websites, content, or analytics work.

Question 1

Why should someone choose you?

We look for clear differentiation, positioning, expertise, proof, and reasons a customer should choose your business instead of another option.

Question 2

Why should they choose you now?

We look for urgency, risk, timing, opportunity cost, seasonal demand, offers, or reasons a customer should act instead of waiting.

Question 3

Why should they trust you?

We review reviews, testimonials, case studies, certifications, photos, team information, process proof, and credibility signals.

Question 4

What happens next?

We review calls to action, contact options, forms, phone visibility, next steps, lead friction, and customer journey clarity.

Question 5

How will success be measured?

We review whether leads, calls, traffic, conversions, revenue, rankings, cost per lead, and growth outcomes can be tracked clearly.

Result

Your highest-impact growth opportunities.

The goal is not to recommend more marketing activity. The goal is to identify what should improve first and how success will be measured.
