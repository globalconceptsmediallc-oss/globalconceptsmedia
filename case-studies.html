Proof of Work

Five marketing leaks we found and fixed.

These examples are anonymous where needed. The point is not name-dropping. The point is showing how hidden problems get found, fixed, and turned into progress.

Case Study 01

Merchant Center Recovery

Product visibility was being blocked by feed issues, product data gaps, and platform warnings.

Problem

Products were active on the site, but Google Shopping visibility was limited because the catalog was not clean enough to compete.

Fix

We reviewed diagnostics, cleaned product data, corrected feed issues, and monitored product eligibility.

Impact

Shopping visibility recovered, product discoverability improved, and the business had a clearer path to revenue from product search.

Result: Product visibility recovered after hidden catalog issues were fixed.

Case Study 02

Analytics Recovery

The business could not trust its reporting because traffic and conversions were not being measured correctly.

Problem

Tracking was missing, duplicated, incomplete, or not firing on the actions that mattered most.

Fix

We audited the live site, verified tags, corrected installation issues, tested events, and confirmed live reporting.

Impact

Reporting confidence improved, conversion visibility returned, and marketing decisions could be made from real data.

Result: The business could finally see what traffic and leads were doing.

Case Study 03

Technical SEO Recovery

Search visibility was being weakened by duplicate URL patterns, redirect issues, sitemap conflicts, and crawl confusion.

Problem

Search engines were receiving mixed signals from redirects, canonicals, internal links, and page structure.

Fix

We cleaned up redirect paths, aligned sitemap URLs, repaired internal links, and normalized canonical signals.

Impact

Crawl paths became cleaner, authority leaks were reduced, and the site became easier for search engines to understand.

Result: Cleaner technical structure with fewer authority leaks.

Case Study 04

Local Visibility Recovery

A local business needed stronger city-level visibility and a clearer connection between services, locations, and customer intent.

Problem

Service pages and city pages were not supporting each other clearly enough, weakening local relevance.

Fix

We expanded location structure, improved internal linking, clarified service coverage, and strengthened crawl paths.

Impact

The site gained stronger local signals and clearer visibility opportunities in target markets.

Result: Stronger local search structure across services and cities.

Case Study 05

Lead Generation Recovery

Paid traffic was active, but the path from visitor to qualified lead was unclear and difficult to measure.

Problem

Campaigns were spending money without enough clarity around search intent, lead quality, conversion actions, and landing page performance.

Fix

We reviewed campaign structure, cleaned conversion goals, checked search terms, and clarified the lead path.

Impact

Wasted spend became easier to reduce, lead quality became easier to judge, and optimization decisions became more confident.

Result: Better visibility into which traffic created real opportunities.

Lead Quality Problems

Traffic coming in without a clear path to qualified calls, forms, or sales opportunities.

Reporting Blind Spots

Business owners making decisions from incomplete, disconnected, or inaccurate data.
