Case Study 01

Merchant Center Recovery

Product visibility was being limited because catalog data and Merchant Center issues were preventing products from showing properly.

What We Found

Products were losing visibility in Google Shopping because of feed issues, product data gaps, and policy warnings.

What We Fixed

We reviewed feed diagnostics, corrected product data, cleaned up Merchant Center issues, and monitored eligibility.

What Changed

Product eligibility improved, Shopping visibility returned, and the store had a clearer path to compete for product searches.

Result

Product visibility recovered.

The business regained access to Shopping traffic that had been quietly blocked by feed and platform issues.

Case Study 02

Analytics Recovery

The business could not trust its reporting because website traffic and conversion activity were not being measured correctly.

What We Found

Analytics tracking was missing, incomplete, duplicated, or not firing where important business activity happened.

What We Fixed

We audited the live site, verified tags, corrected the installation path, tested events, and confirmed live reporting.

What Changed

Reporting confidence was restored, conversion visibility improved, and decisions could be made from real data.

Result

Reporting confidence restored.

The business could finally see whether traffic, leads, and conversions were being tracked correctly.

Case Study 03

Technical SEO Recovery

Search visibility was being weakened by crawl confusion, broken URL patterns, redirects, and indexing problems.

What We Found

Authority was leaking through redirect loops, duplicate URLs, bad canonicals, sitemap conflicts, broken pages, and crawl traps.

What We Fixed

We cleaned up redirects, normalized canonical signals, aligned the sitemap, repaired internal links, and reduced crawl confusion.

What Changed

Search engines had a cleaner path through the site, authority was better protected, and site health became easier to maintain.

Result

Authority leaks reduced.

Crawl paths became cleaner, indexing became more consistent, and the site was easier for search engines to understand.

Case Study 04

Local Visibility Recovery

A local business needed stronger city-level visibility and a clearer service structure for customers and search engines.

What We Found

Service pages and city pages were not supporting each other clearly enough, weakening local relevance.

What We Fixed

We expanded local page structure, strengthened service-to-city linking, improved crawl paths, and clarified geographic relevance.

What Changed

The site gained stronger local signals, better service coverage, and clearer visibility opportunities in target markets.

Result

Local relevance strengthened.

The business had a clearer local search structure connecting services, cities, and customer intent.

Case Study 05

Lead Generation Recovery

Paid traffic was active, but the path from visitor to qualified lead was unclear and difficult to measure.

What We Found

Campaigns were spending money without enough clarity around search intent, lead quality, conversion actions, and landing page performance.

What We Fixed

We reviewed campaign structure, cleaned up conversion goals, improved tracking, reviewed search terms, and clarified the lead path.

What Changed

Wasted spend was reduced, lead quality became easier to evaluate, and optimization decisions became more confident.

Result

Lead quality became easier to measure.

The business could better understand which traffic sources were creating real sales opportunities.
